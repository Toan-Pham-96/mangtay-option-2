About us

Nha Ho Asparagus is owned by Nghe An Agricultural Materials JSC, with more than 60 years of farming experience. We currently are the biggest asparagus grower in Viet Nam.

In addition to the general climate characteristics of Ninh Thuan province as high radiation level and low air humidity to suit the growth process of asparagus, our farm is an isolate terrain which surrounded by Song Cai- Phan Rang river. The secret lies in the unique soil in the region that is not only highly fertile but organic, and cant be resembled anywhere else in other farms. Moreover, it also reduces the crops diseases as a result.

Asparagus is packed full of nutrients, and a very good source of vitamins and antioxidants such as A, B, C, K, E, and folate. It contains minerals such as potassium, magnesium and iron, and is a good source of dietary fibre. We saw how asparagus brings the nutritional contents and possible health benefits to everyone and its opportunity in the market.

The business decided to dedicate the land solely to the production of asparagus as the export market was booming and local demand on the rise.

Our products

“Non-GMO, and offer full traceability back to the farm.”

Nghe An Agricultural Materials JSC has accelerated the construction of an organic green asparagus production line with the participation of more than 200 production workers and 44 technical staffs. All of our members strictly follow the farming process which is requested by specialists. We applied technology to improve product quality with a standard nursery system- total area of ​​7000m2 covered with a portable PE film with sunscreen that can adjust lighting levels, has a system of watering and spraying rain. Our field is drip irrigated, with aquifer water from our private wells, and are located directly adjacent to our packing facilities. Moreover, organic fertilizer is also applied in order to enhance the asparagus quality. The asparagus is harvested and sent immediately to our facilities where it is washed clean, sorted , trimmed, packed and cooled. We strive to take the utmost care and safety in how we operate and the standards we adhere to. Our goal is to partner with the best produce professionals in every market. We can pack to any specification you desire and look forward to working with you.
